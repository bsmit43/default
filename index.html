Crafting Quality Wood Products Since 1985

At Wood Rift, we are passionate about transforming raw wood into beautiful and functional products. With over 35 years of experience, our team of skilled artisans combines traditional techniques with modern technology to create pieces that are both durable and aesthetically pleasing.

We source only the finest sustainably harvested hardwoods, ensuring that our products are not only of the highest quality but also environmentally responsible. From custom furniture and cabinetry to architectural millwork and specialty wood items, we offer a wide range of services to meet your unique needs.

Our commitment to customer satisfaction is unwavering. We work closely with each client to understand their vision and bring it to life with precision and care. Whether you're a homeowner, architect, or contractor, you can trust Wood Rift to deliver exceptional craftsmanship and unparalleled service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wood products and services.

What types of wood do you use?

We primarily use sustainably sourced hardwoods such as oak, maple, cherry, and walnut. We also offer products made from reclaimed wood, giving new life to materials with a rich history. Specific wood types are detailed in the product descriptions.

Are your products environmentally friendly?

Yes, sustainability is a core value at Wood Rift. We source our wood from responsibly managed forests and use eco-friendly finishes whenever possible. We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact.

Do you offer custom wood products?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom wood pieces tailored to your specific needs and design preferences. Contact us to discuss your project and receive a personalized quote.

What is the lead time for orders?

Lead times vary depending on the complexity and size of the order. In-stock items typically ship within 3-5 business days. Custom orders may take 4-6 weeks. We will provide an estimated delivery date when you place your order.

What are your shipping options?

We offer a variety of shipping options, including standard ground, expedited shipping, and freight shipping for larger items. Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight and destination of your order.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, you may return it within 30 days for a full refund (excluding shipping costs). Custom orders may not be eligible for return. Please see our full return policy for details.

Still have questions? Contact us and we'll be happy to help.
