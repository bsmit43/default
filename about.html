About test

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Sarah Johnson, test began as a small virtual assistant service with a big vision: to empower businesses by handling their administrative burdens, allowing them to focus on growth and innovation. Sarah, a seasoned executive assistant with over 15 years of experience, recognized the growing need for reliable, efficient, and cost-effective administrative support.

Initially serving local startups, test quickly gained a reputation for excellence and expanded its services to include bookkeeping, HR support, and project management. Our commitment to personalized service and cutting-edge technology has enabled us to build long-lasting relationships with clients across diverse industries.

Today, test is a leading provider of comprehensive administrative solutions, serving businesses of all sizes. We are proud of our journey and remain dedicated to providing exceptional support that helps our clients thrive.
