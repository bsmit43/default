Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a wide range of materials including PLA, ABS, PETG, Nylon, and flexible TPU. We also provide specialized materials like carbon fiber reinforced filaments and resins for high-resolution printing.
We primarily accept STL, OBJ, and 3MF file formats. For complex projects, we can also work with STEP and IGES files. Please ensure your files are properly oriented and scaled before submission.
Our printers have a maximum build volume of 300mm x 300mm x 400mm. For larger projects, we can print in multiple parts and assemble them.
Turnaround time depends on the complexity and size of the project. Simple prints can be completed within 24-48 hours, while more complex projects may take 3-5 business days. We will provide an estimated timeline upon receiving your design.
