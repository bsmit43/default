Frequently Asked Questions

What types of wood do you use?

We primarily use sustainably sourced hardwoods like oak, maple, cherry, and walnut. We also offer options in reclaimed wood for a unique, rustic look. All our wood is carefully selected for its quality and durability.

Do you offer custom woodworking services?

Yes, we specialize in custom woodworking projects. Whether you need a unique piece of furniture, custom cabinetry, or architectural millwork, our skilled craftsmen can bring your vision to life. Contact us to discuss your project requirements.

What is the lead time for custom orders?

Lead times for custom orders vary depending on the complexity of the project and our current workload. We will provide you with an estimated lead time during the initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

How do I care for my wood furniture?

To maintain the beauty and longevity of your wood furniture, we recommend dusting regularly with a soft cloth and avoiding exposure to direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. For spills, wipe immediately with a damp cloth and dry thoroughly. We also offer specialized wood care products.

Do you ship your products?

Yes, we ship our products nationwide. We carefully package each item to ensure it arrives safely at your doorstep. Shipping costs vary depending on the size and weight of the item and the destination. We provide tracking information for all shipments.

What is your return policy?

We stand behind the quality of our products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Custom orders are non-refundable. Please contact us for return instructions.

Are your finishes eco-friendly?

We are committed to using eco-friendly finishes whenever possible. We offer a range of low-VOC and water-based finishes that are safe for your home and the environment. We can provide details on specific finishes upon request.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services for our cabinetry and millwork products. Our experienced installers will ensure that your project is completed to the highest standards. Contact us for a quote.

How can I get a quote for a custom project?

To get a quote for a custom project, please contact us with details about your project, including dimensions, materials, and design preferences. You can email us, call us, or visit our showroom. We will provide you with a detailed quote as soon as possible.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders. Please contact us with details about your order, including the quantity and types of products you are interested in. We will provide you with a custom quote.
